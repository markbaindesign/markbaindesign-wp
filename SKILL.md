## Quick rules
- Monospace-first: JetBrains Mono for everything, IBM Plex Mono for code/metadata, Source Serif only for rare editorial moments.
- Square corners. `border-radius: 0` is load-bearing.
- Warm cream `#F5F0E8` paper, ink `#141413`, clay `#C96442` accent. Links stay blue `#1A4FE3`, always underlined.
- Use `[bracketed]` metadata, `/` slash separators, `✔` phosphor-green bullets, blinking `_` cursors, and a `♥` for the "Build with ♥" sign-off.
- No emoji. No glassmorphism/blur. No aggressive gradients. No rounded corners.
- Voice: first-person, British English, sentence case, plain verbs ("Arrange a chat now"). Friendly and technical.
